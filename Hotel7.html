 Overview

 Hotel 7 is close to popular beaches and tourist attractions.It is 60 minutes away from the airport and 35 minutes

 indoor and al fresco seating that serves a selection of Indian and international cuisine for lunch and dinner along
 with spirits and delicious cocktails. At fixed schedules, the hotel provides complimentary shuttle services from  
 the hotel to the beach.
